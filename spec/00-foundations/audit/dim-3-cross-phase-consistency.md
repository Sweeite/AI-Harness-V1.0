# Dimension 3 — Cross-phase Consistency

This dimension audited whether artifacts produced across different phases of the spec (Phase 1 requirements in `spec/01-requirements/`, Phase 2 config in `spec/02-config/config-registry.md`, Phase 3 surfaces in `spec/03-surfaces/`, Phase 4 data model in `spec/04-data-model/schema.md` and related files, and Phase 5 non-functional requirements in `spec/05-non-functional/`) actually agree with one another once a later phase supersedes, renames, splits, or reclassifies something an earlier phase already cited. Checks included: verifying that `DATA-` field/table citations in FRs still resolve against the authoritative Phase-4 schema (including post-split renames such as the credentials-table split and entity-type modeling); verifying that config-key citations and their edit-class assumptions (LIVE/BOOT) match the Phase-2 config-registry and its taxonomy standard; verifying that permission-node citations for gating a given config/workflow agree between the owning component FR and the authoritative registry section that owns those keys; verifying that surface-level (Phase 3) data-binding tables and section key-lists stay in sync with later Phase-2/Phase-4 additions; and verifying that feasibility-register (AF-) and NFR/test-strategy citations correctly describe the claim they point to. This is the working evidence file (pre-adversarial-verification) — findings below are rendered as reported by the dimension's agents, without editorializing on which will survive verification.
